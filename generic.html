About Me

Hi, I’m Ntombiyokuthula Khwela. I’m a data analyst with 2 years of hands-on experience, and a deep passion for using data to solve real-world problems. My journey into data analytics started with a genuine curiosity about how businesses make decisions — and I quickly realized that data is at the heart of it all. I was drawn to the way raw numbers can tell a powerful story when organized and analyzed the right way. That’s what sparked my passion, and I haven’t looked back since. Over the past couple of years, I’ve worked on projects in retail, healthcare, and business operations — using tools like SQL, Excel, and Power BI to clean, analyze, and visualize data in ways that make sense and add value. I'm currently expanding my skills through the ALX Africa Data Science Certificate, where I’m learning more about Python, advanced analytics, and the broader field of data science. I’m excited to keep growing, and to take on projects that challenge me to think deeper and build better solutions.
Thanks for stopping by — feel free to explore my projects or reach out if you’d like to connect!

Becoming a data analyst hasn’t been easy, but it’s been incredibly rewarding. As a self-taught analyst, I started with YouTube tutorials, free resources, and late nights trying to make sense of things others learned in classrooms. I had to be resourceful, disciplined, and patient with myself — especially when I didn’t have access to formal training early on. Every challenge I faced, from debugging my first SQL query to building my first dashboard, pushed me to learn more and keep going. That dedication is what shaped my skills and work ethic today. I believe that growth comes from persistence, and I bring that same mindset into every project I work on.

“I may have started with nothing but curiosity and free tutorials — but with consistency, passion, and purpose, I’m building something meaningful one dataset at a time.”
